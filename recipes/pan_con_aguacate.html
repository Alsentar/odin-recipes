Como hacer pan con aguacate

The pan con aguacate is a very easy dish to make. A great breakfast, light lunch or even light dinner. The pan con aguacate combines the slightly sweet taste of bread with the comfortably salty taste of the avocado, making it a perfect simple snack.

Ingredients:

    Bread Avocado

Optional ingredients:

    Eggs

Steps to make:

    Toast the bread Cut the avocado in half For the first half of the avocado, cut it in slices Take the peel off the slices Put the slices on a bowl and mash them together until they're a paste Put the paste on the bread Take the other half of the avocado and cut it in slices Peel the slices, and serve them on top of the paste on the bread Salt and pepper at your leisure Enjoy
